## Implementation

`mql5/MomentumPullbackEA.mq5` now implements:

- version 1.30 and a renamed `InpTakeProfitAtrMultV130=2.0` input, preventing
  the resident chart's saved `InpTakeProfitAtrMult=3.0` value from overriding
  the new default by name;
- a bid-checked long / ask-checked short +1R trigger, where 1R is the position's
  frozen signal-bar Wilder ATR multiplied by `InpStopAtrMult`;
- a 50% close of original volume, floored to broker lot step and skipped rather
  than rounded up when the minimum/remainder is invalid;
- hedging-mode enforcement, bounded retcode retries, and reconciliation before
  retry;
- order-ticket signal ATR and position-identifier partial state persisted through
  terminal global variables, flushed after updates, and reconciled through live
  order/position history on restart;
- authoritative signal ATR protected from later fill-time fallbacks, including
  residual working orders and terminal-offline fills;
- fragmented entries use volume-weighted entry price and cumulative volume; a
  previously infeasible half-lot target re-arms only when later fills make it
  feasible;
- partial `DEAL_ENTRY_OUT` classification that does not stamp a full-exit
  cooldown, close the trade record, or increment the consecutive-loss streak;
- aggregate final trade P/L and R across partial and final exit deals;
- `MomentumPullback_partials_v130.csv`, versioned decision telemetry, and panel
  partial-state output.
