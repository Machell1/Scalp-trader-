## Stage flow and gates

**Stage A (tape families — filters/regime/geometry/exits).** Each cell
produces a variant trade population on the 4-symbol tape.
- Overlay cells (subset of control trades): statistic = E2 mean-R gap
  (kept − vetoed); p from 10,000 within-symbol permutations of the veto flag.
- Variant cells (different trades — geometry/exits): statistic = E2 pooled
  expectancy delta vs control; p from 2,000 day-clustered block bootstraps.
- A cell survives Stage A only if ALL of: OOS E2 delta > 0; full-sample E2
  delta ≥ +0.02R (house materiality bar, below the +0.03 session bar to admit
  borderline cells into FDR rather than pre-filtering); conditioned/variant
  n ≥ 50; and its p-value survives **Benjamini-Hochberg FDR at 10% computed
  across ALL Stage-A cells run**. Grid families (depth/window/lookback) apply
  their pre-stated plateau requirement BEFORE entering FDR (a family enters as
  its single plateau-consistent candidate or not at all).
- Cap: at most the **top 5** survivors by OOS delta advance to Stage B.

**Stage B (screen MC).** Survivors are wired into the account tape (overlay =
trade removal; geometry/exit = re-resolved events) and run at 20,000 common
paths vs the deployed control. Gates (identical to USDJPY/XAUUSD admissions):
both-phases point AND Wilson lower ≥ 78.887%; exact paired one-sided 95%
lower delta vs deployed control > 0; hard-halt ≤ 1%; timeout ≤ control.

**Sizing/portfolio family skips Stage A** (cells do not change trades): all
its RUN cells go straight into ONE 20k-path multi-policy screen sharing common
paths, same five gates, and at most the single best passer by paired lower
advances (ties → simpler rule).

**Stage C.** Each Stage-B passer (max 5 tape + 1 sizing) confirmed at 100,000
paths, same gates. If ≥2 confirm, a COMBINED configuration must also pass a
final 100k paired run before any multi-cell proposal (interaction control).

**Outcome handling.** Full results table committed regardless. Failed cells
are closed on this data. Passed cells = v1.32 proposal for owner sign-off,
default-OFF inputs, forward-demo validation before any challenge decision.
